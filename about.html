About 🐾 PawTrace

🐾 PawTrace is a compassionate online platform built to connect pet owners, rescuers, and neighborhoods so missing pets can return home safely.

Our Mission

To speed up pet recovery through instant alerts, transparent reporting, and local community involvement.

Our Vision

A world where every missing pet has a reliable digital support network for safe and quick reunions.

How It Works

1. Report

Submit pet details, last seen location, and contact info in minutes.

2. Alert

Your report appears in alerts for nearby people and animal helpers.

3. Reunite

Track progress and connect with sightings until your pet comes home.
